FAQ

What ages do you accept?

  • Our core programs are designed for ages 5–14.
  • Competitions and showcases are open to all enrolled students.

Do kids need prior experience?

  • No. We start from basics and group by experience level.
  • Advanced tracks are available for returning learners.

What equipment is required?

  • We provide devices and robotics kits for classes.
  • Home practice uses free, child‑friendly platforms.
